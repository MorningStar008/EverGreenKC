About Us


Since 2023, we have been committed to enhancing our community's well-being by planting trees and fostering green spaces throughout the Kansas City area. With each tree we plant, we envision a cleaner, healthier environment for generations to come. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to educate, advocate, and engage with the KC community to promote eco-conscious practices and conservation efforts. Together, we strive to preserve the natural beauty of Kansas City while mitigating the effects of climate change. Join us in our mission to create a greener, more vibrant tomorrow for all.
