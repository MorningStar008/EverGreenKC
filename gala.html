Annual Go Green Gala


When?


July 28, 2024

3-7:30pm

Where?


Powell Gardens, Kansas City, Mo


1609 NW US Hwy 50, Kingsville, MO 64061

What?


Fun activities to connect with nature and plant trees!


Activities include walks, marathons, games, music, food, raffles, and more!


What's the Schedule?


Time: Activity/Event: Description:
2PM Guest Check-In Opening speaker will introduce guests to the gala and provide information about nature scavenger hunt, raffle, auction, and Child's Garden for families.
3:30PM Walks/Runs Start Guests have the option to particpate in the nature scavenger hunt, 1 mile casual walk, or a 3.25 mile running competition. The scavenger hunt will take place at the Child's Garden, Visitor Center, and Fountain Garden. The walk and running competition will take place at the Bryon Schutz Trail System. Runners can receive a certificate of participation and awards for first, second, and third place.
4:15PM Walks/Runs/Scavenger Hunt Ends Guests will return for food and performances
4:30PM Food & Performances Begin Guests may choose a vegan or non-vegan burger for food provided by catering. Guests are responsible for choosing food based on dietary restrictions and allergies. Performances will happen as people eat.
5:30PM Tree Planting Begins Adults will be placed in groups and plant trees in different garden spots. Children will be craft terrariums. EverGreenKC volunteers will be nearby for assistance.
7:00PM Raffle, Auction, and Participation Prizes Tree planting will end and raffle, auction, and participation prizes will be awarded.
7:30PM Closing Keynote Closing speaker will give a speech and end the day.
